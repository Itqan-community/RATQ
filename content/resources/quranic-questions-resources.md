# Quranic Questions Resources

This file contains a list of providers to Quranic question banks, you can use in your app. Some of the resources are free and some need subscription, so getting the access to these resource is up to you.

> **NOTE**: I didn't review the answers of the provided questions, so their validation is up to you. Make sure to validate them before using them.

1. [https://khuddam.org.uk/](https://khuddam.org.uk/)
    
    An organization based in UK and aims to present the peaceful message of Islam and removing common misconceptions and misunderstandings about the faith.

    One of its resource is a pdf containing more than 380 quranic questions. You can find the pdf here: [https://khuddam.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/Quran-Quiz-Questions-1.pdf](https://khuddam.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/Quran-Quiz-Questions-1.pdf)

2. [https://www.scribd.com/](https://www.scribd.com/)

    A platform for reading and sharing documents, books, and magazines. It is not specific for quranic questions or even Islamic content, but I found many resources that contain many quranic questions (and islamic questions, in general). In order to be able to download them , you need subscription of 10$/mo with free trial, or you upload 3 documents to be able to download one.

    Some examples are:

    a. [Qur'an Quiz for Islamic Scholars](https://www.scribd.com/document/591611062/1-QURAN-QUIZ-1-ENG-ARAB)
    b. [Quran Knowledge Challenge](https://www.scribd.com/document/591610617/2-QURAN-QUIZ-2-ENG-ARAB)
    c. [QURAN](https://www.scribd.com/document/909038554/QURAN)
    d. [100 Facts of The Quran](https://www.scribd.com/document/909028758/100-Facts-of-the-Quran)
    e. [100 Questions Answers On Holy Quran](https://www.scribd.com/document/19537447/100-questions-answers-on-holy-quran)
    
    And many more.

3. [General knowledge of the Quran and Islam for kids](https://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/general-knowledge-of-the-quran-and-islam-for-kids/37717605)

    This document provides 57 questions and answers about general knowledge of the Quran and Islam. It covers topics like the number of surahs and verses in the Quran, important people, places, events, rulings, and stories mentioned in the Quran and early Islamic history. The questions are multiple choice or short answer format and touch on topics ranging from the basic structure of the Quran to details of the Prophet's life and early Islamic caliphates.

    > You can surf [slideshare.net](https://www.slideshare.net/) to find more resources.

4. [NOOR ACADEMY](https://nooracademy.com/)

    Noor Academy is an online Islamic learning platform offering Quran classes, Arabic lessons, and Islamic Studies for kids ages 5–18. Our mission is to provide an accessible, engaging, and high-quality education in the Quran, Tajweed, Islamic Studies, and Arabic language, especially for families who may face challenges accessing traditional Islamic schools.

    They provide some examples of the questions on this page -> [https://nooracademy.com/quran-quiz-for-children/](https://nooracademy.com/quran-quiz-for-children/)

5. [Understand Al-Qur’an Academy](https://understandquran.com/downloads/)

    An academy provides many courses in teaching and understanding Quran. They provide downloadable resource for each course in many languages, in which you can find many questions (but not MCQ) at the end of each pdf, you can get some inspirations from them or use them as you see fit.
