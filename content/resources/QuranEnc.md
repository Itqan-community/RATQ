# QuranEnc

## Overview

[QuranEnc](https://quranenc.com/en/home) is a portal featuring free and trustworthy translations of the meanings and exegeses of the noble Qur'an in many world languages.
These renditions have been prepared by reliable translators under the careful supervision of specialized bodies. They are both accessible and shareable on social media.

## Key Features

- They provide reliable translations of the meanings of the Qur'an, based on the methodology of Ahlus-Sunnah wal-Jama'ah, as an alternative to untrustworthy online sources.
- It provides translations of the Quran for 79 different languages.
- Each language has multiple translations provided by different translators.
- The translations are provided in different formats, API, CSV, XLS, XML, and PDF. Which suits different use cases.
- It also provides a web applcation, android app, and iOS app for browsing the Quran and its preferred translations.
