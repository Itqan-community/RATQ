# Quran Foundation

[Quran.Foundation](https://quran.foundation/) is a deeply committed, purpose-driven non-profit organization dedicated to building platforms and supporting projects that aim to transform every human through Quranic guidance by making the Quran accessible and helping people engage deeply with its teachings through digital platforms.

## Who runs it?

Quran.Foundation is run by a board of directors and trustees responsible for its governance and management. The board includes members such as Amer Abbas, Nour Sharabash, Ayman Hammous, and Mohamed Sadeia, who oversee the nonprofit's activities, including its flagship website Quran.com. The organization conducts formal board orientation and assessments to ensure governance standards.

## Platforms

1. [Quran.com](../../content/apps/quran.com.md)

   Quran.com is a web application designed to make the Quran accessible to everyone by providing translations, tafsir (exegesis), recitations, and word-by-word translations. It offers various tools for deeper Quranic study in multiple languages, making it easier to understand the Quran's meanings.

2. [Quran.com-API](../../content/resources/quran.com-api.md)

   Quran.com-API is a RESTful API that is designed to empower Muslim App developers to build on top of the same backend that powers Quran Foundation apps. These APIs provide the functionality and content on Quran.com and QuranReflect, facilitating easy access to comprehensive ayah-and-surah-level content. User authentication enhances the App's functionality, enabling the sync of bookmarks, notes, reflections, reading history, streaks, and more. By integrating with this APIs users can enjoy a rich connected experience across Quranic apps, and you can focus on innovating unique solutions that the world needs.

3. [Quran.com-Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.quran.labs.androidquran)

   The Quran.com Android app brings the Quran to millions of users worldwide, offering a seamless and intuitive experience for reading, listening, and understanding the Quran. Designed for ease of use, the app provides powerful tools to enhance engagement, including translations, recitations, and tafsir. Developeres woking on this project continue to refine and expand its features, ensuring that the Quran is always accessible at your fingertips.

4. [Quran.com-iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/quran-by-quran-com-%D9%82%D8%B1%D8%A2%D9%86/id1118663303)

   The Quran.com iOS app brings the trusted experience of Quran.com to your fingertips, offering a seamless and intuitive way to read, explore, and understand the Quran. Designed for ease of use and deep engagement, the app provides access to authentic Quranic text, translations, and study tools anytime, anywhere. Developeres woking on this project continue to enhance the app with new features, ensuring a smooth and enriching experience for users seeking a deeper connection with the Quran.

5. [QuranReflect](https://quranreflect.com/)

   QuranReflect is a web application designed to provide a platform for users to create and share reflections on Quranic verses. It offers a user-friendly interface for users to write, edit, and publish reflections on Quranic verses, as well as a search and discovery feature to find and explore reflections created by other users. The application also provides a community feature for users to connect with other users and engage in discussions about Quranic reflections. Alongside lessons from qualified teachers, personal insights are welcomed that undergo expert quality review, led by Shaykh Hammad Fahim and our resident scholar Dr. Sohaib Saeed. This platform mission is to inspire meaningful conversations and deepen the world’s relationship with the Book of Allah.

6. [Corpus.Quran.com](https://corpus.quran.com/)

   Corpus.Quran.com is an annotated linguistic resource which shows the Arabic grammar, syntax and morphology for each word in the Holy Quran. The corpus provides three levels of analysis: morphological annotation, a syntactic treebank and a semantic ontology.

7. [sunnah.com](https://sunnah.com/about)

   sunnah.com is a platform designed to make authentic, comprehensive, and beneficial information pertaining to the sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad (saws) accessible to as many people around the world as possible in order to facilitate research and promote its mainstream and broadly accepted understanding.

8. [Nuqayah.com](https://nuqayah.com/)

   Nuqayah is an organization that provides many projects that are related to the Quran and the Sunnah such as الباحث القرآني، المقرئ، التفسير التفاعلي, and many other projects.
